A note on salary

"Salary" can either be hourly, weekly, monthly, or yearly as long as salary units of starting and target remain the same.

E.g. It's okay to use a starting salary of

  • A starting salary of 30000/year and target salary of 50000/year
  • starting of 12/hour and target of 15/hour

What won't work is mismatched units, such as starting of 12/hour and target of 30000/year.
