Raise Goal

This page estimates the constant percentage rate you need per year to get you from your starting salary to your target salary by a certain year.

Note: "Salary" can either be hourly, weekly, monthly, or yearly as long as salary units of starting and target remain the same.
E.g. It's okay to use a starting salary of 30000/year and target salary of 50000/year, or starting of 12/hour and target of 15/hour.
What won't work is mismatched units, such as starting of 12/hour and target of 30000/year.
